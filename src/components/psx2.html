About PSX Battle of Bulls

PSX Battle of Bulls is a highly engaging and educational virtual trading contest that allows participants to practice stock market trading without any real financial risk. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced trader, this contest provides the perfect platform to test your strategies, learn from your decisions, and enhance your investment knowledge.

Our virtual stock market tool can be used in classrooms to educate students about investing or by individuals looking to refine their trading skills.

Competition terms and conditions

1. The Competition

This competition is a prized competition draw operated by or on behalf of the Pakistan Stock Exchange (PSX) to promote PSX’s My Portfolio Trading Simulation application (the “Competition”).

2. Territory and eligibility

  • The Competition is open to residents of Pakistan aged 18 years and over. Competition Participants and then later on Winners will need to provide a Pakistan Identity number to prove they are residents of Pakistan.
  • Only one entry per person is allowed. No participant may win more than one prize.
  • Entry to the Competition is free of charge although participants are responsible for their own costs of connecting to the Internet.
  • Competition isn’t allowed if your next of kin is working in the Pakistani brokerage industry.

  • Competition winners will be notified via PSX’s website, PSX’s Facebook and Twitter Pages. In this notification, Pakistan Stock Exchange will inform each Competition winner of how to claim their prize and of any additional information or steps required to enable PSX to provide them with the prize. Pakistan Stock Exchange reserves the right to award the relevant prize to another Competition participant if the prize is not claimed within 3 days after such a notification.

4. How to enter

The Competition will be promoted through Pakistan Stock Exchange website, its Facebook page and from its twitter page account.

To enter participants must follow these steps:

  • Log into PSX website’s My Portfolio page.
  • Press Register for Competition Button.
  • Input your required details in the given menu and press Register after which you will receive a confirmatory email.
  • After clicking on confirmatory email sent at your email. You’re ready to participate in the competition. Good luck!
